Goulash

Serves 4

This Hungarian classic is a comforting Winter Warmer!

Ingredients

  • 800-1,000g Beef Chuck
  • 2 cups White Rice
  • 2 Onions, diced
  • 500ml Beef Stock
  • 500g White Potatoes, peeled
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 tbsp Ghee

Seasonings

  • 3 tbsp Hungarian Paprika
  • 1 tsp Thyme (or Marjoram), Caraway Seeds, Black Pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Optional

  • Sour Cream

Instructions

  1. Turn on an InstantPot's Sautee function, heat up the ghee, and begin to brown the meat.
  2. Once browned, remove the meat and add the diced onions.
  3. When the onions are carmelised, add a splash of beef stock, add the seasonings and scrap the "brown bits" (the fond) off the bottom.
  4. After 30 seconds, return the beef to the pot and set to pressure cook at high for 35 minutes (with NPR of 15 minutes).
  5. Once the NPR has begun, put the uncooked white rice into a rice cooker. Two cups of rice usually requires 3 cups of water (2:3).
