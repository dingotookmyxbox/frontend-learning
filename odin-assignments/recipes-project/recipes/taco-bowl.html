Taco Bowl

Serves 2

A quick and easy beef mince recipe that mimics the taste and flavour of those El Paso Taco Seasoning packs.

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 2 cups White Rice
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 3 tbsp Water
  • 1 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar

Seasonings

  • 2 tsp Cumin and Paprika
  • 1 tsp Oregano, Onion Powder, Black Pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Optional

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa

Instructions

  1. Put the uncooked white rice into a rice cooker. Two cups of rice usually requires 3 cups of water (2:3).
  2. Brown the mince over medium-high heat.
  3. Once browned, added the seasonings. Stir until the mince is evenly coated.
  4. Add the tomato paste, water and ACV, and stir until thoroughly mixed.
  5. Take the meat off the heat, the rice should be ready now - serve with your choice of optional ingredients!
